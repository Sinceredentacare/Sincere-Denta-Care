Our Services

From routine check-ups to complex implant procedures — we offer 12 specialized dental services tailored to your every need.

Routine Dental Check-ups & Cleaning

Regular dental visits are the cornerstone of excellent oral health. Our comprehensive check-up includes a thorough examination of your teeth, gums, and jaw, followed by professional scaling and polishing to remove plaque, tartar, and surface stains. Early detection of any issues helps prevent costly treatments later.

  • Full mouth examination
  • Professional scaling & polishing
  • X-ray analysis (if needed)
  • Personalized oral hygiene advice

Dental Implant

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. A titanium post is surgically placed into the jawbone, acting as an artificial root, upon which a natural-looking crown is placed. The result is a permanent, functional, and aesthetic tooth replacement that can last a lifetime with proper care.

  • Titanium implant placement
  • Custom porcelain crowns
  • Full mouth implant solutions
  • 3D smile planning
Book This Service

Root Canal Treatment

Modern root canal treatment is virtually painless and can save a severely infected or damaged tooth. Our specialists use rotary endodontic tools and precise imaging to clean, shape, and seal the root canals, eliminating infection and restoring the tooth to full health — all in as few as one or two visits.

  • Single & multi-visit RCT
  • Rotary endodontics
  • Pain-free procedure
  • Post-treatment crown placement
Book This Service

Orthodontics

Straighten your teeth and correct your bite with our orthodontic solutions. We offer metal braces, ceramic braces, and lingual braces, customized for children, teens, and adults. Properly aligned teeth not only look better but are also easier to clean, reducing the risk of decay and gum disease.

  • Metal & ceramic braces
  • Lingual (hidden) braces
  • Bite correction
  • Retainer fabrication
Book This Service

Clear Aligners

Achieve a perfectly straight smile without anyone knowing you're wearing braces. Clear aligners are custom-fabricated, removable trays that gradually shift your teeth into their ideal positions. They are virtually invisible, comfortable to wear, and easy to remove for eating and brushing.

  • Virtually invisible trays
  • Removable & hygienic
  • Digital treatment planning
  • Suitable for teens & adults
Book This Service

Filling for Cavities

Tooth-coloured composite fillings restore decayed or damaged teeth to their natural appearance and function. Unlike old amalgam fillings, our composite resin fillings bond directly to the tooth structure, requiring minimal removal of healthy tooth tissue and providing a seamless, natural look.

  • Tooth-coloured composite
  • Glass ionomer fillings
  • Minimal invasive approach
  • Same-day treatment
Book This Service

Pediatric Dental Care

We create a warm, friendly, and stress-free environment for your little ones. Our pediatric dental specialists are trained to handle children of all ages with patience and care. From the first tooth to teenage years, we monitor growth, prevent decay, and educate children about good oral habits early.

  • Child-friendly approach
  • Preventive sealants & fluoride
  • Habit counselling
  • Milk tooth treatments

Dental Prosthesis

Restore your smile and chewing ability with our range of dental prostheses. We offer removable and fixed options including complete dentures, partial dentures, and implant-supported prostheses. Each prosthesis is carefully crafted to match the colour, shape, and size of your natural teeth.

  • Complete & partial dentures
  • Flexible dentures
  • Implant-retained prostheses
  • Immediate dentures
Book This Service

Gum Treatments

Healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy smile. We provide a full range of periodontal treatments including deep cleaning (scaling & root planing), gum flap surgery, and gum recontouring. Early treatment of gum disease can prevent tooth loss and has been linked to improved overall health.

  • Deep cleaning (SRP)
  • Gum flap surgery
  • Gum recession treatment
  • Laser gum therapy
Book This Service

Wisdom Tooth Removal

Impacted or painful wisdom teeth can cause significant discomfort and crowding. Our oral surgeons perform safe and efficient wisdom tooth extractions using modern surgical techniques and local anaesthesia, ensuring minimal discomfort during and after the procedure. After-care guidance is provided thoroughly.

  • Simple & surgical extractions
  • Impacted tooth removal
  • IV sedation available
  • Detailed post-op care

  • Professional teeth whitening
  • Porcelain veneers
